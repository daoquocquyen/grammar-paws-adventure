## Idea Organization and Prioritization

### Product vision (short)

A cozy web adventure where kids learn grammar through playful 2–5 minute quests, earning meaningful pet customizations and evolution milestones for topic mastery.

### Target player profile

- 11-year-old non-native English learner
- Enjoys fantasy/pet care and quick game sessions
- Needs clear feedback, low frustration, and visible progress
- Benefits from confidence-building and parent-visible progress summaries

### Story fantasy (1 paragraph)

In Grammar Paws Adventure, a young explorer discovers a magical pet egg in the Library Forest, where words shape the world. Each time the explorer helps their pet solve sentence puzzles, restore punctuation constellations, and cross preposition paths, the pet grows stronger and the world brightens. Mastering grammar topics unlocks enchanted accessories and evolution keys, turning learning progress into visible adventure progress. The child is never punished for mistakes—only guided forward with gentle hints and celebratory wins.

### Core mechanics list

- 2–5 minute quest loop: prompt → mini-game → instant feedback → reward
- Topic mastery model: Learn → Practice → Master
- 4 reusable mini-game templates re-skinned across topics
- Accessory rewards for each completed topic (player chooses reward)
- Evolution milestones every two completed topics
- Adaptive hints and retry support without penalty
- Parent-friendly weekly progress digest

### Grammar-topic roadmap (first 8 topics)

1. Nouns (common/proper)
2. Articles (a/an/the)
3. Pronouns (subject/object basics)
4. Verb “to be” + present simple
5. Adjectives (describing nouns)
6. Prepositions of place
7. Present continuous vs simple present (intro)
8. Basic punctuation (period, question mark, comma)

### Reward economy

| Trigger | Reward Type | Child Choice | Evolution Impact | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Complete one topic | Accessory chest | Pick 1 of 3 | +1 mastery point | Always granted |
| Master rating on topic | Premium cosmetic variant | Yes | +2 mastery points | Cosmetic only |
| Two topics completed | Evolution key | N/A | Stage unlock | Predictable milestone |
| 7-day healthy streak (optional) | Sticker page | Yes | None | No punitive reset pressure |
| Checkpoint boss cleared | Room decoration | Yes | +1 milestone bonus | Celebration moment |

### MVP scope

**Must-have**
- One pet with 4 evolution stages
- 8 grammar topics
- 4 mini-game templates
- Learn/Practice/Master structure
- Topic completion rewards + accessory equip UI
- Basic adaptive hints and retry
- Simple progress dashboard for child + weekly parent digest

**Should-have**
- Mood check before session
- Bridge quests across old/new topics
- Lightweight checkpoint boss puzzle every 2 topics

**Later**
- Multiple pet species
- Social/co-op features
- Voice interactions
- Seasonal events and themed reward tracks

### Success metrics (learning + engagement)

- Topic completion rate (per week)
- Mastery rate by topic (first-pass and after hints)
- Average session length (target: 2–5 min)
- Return rate (D1, D7) without unhealthy streak dependence
- Hint usage trend (should decline over time per topic)
- Frustration signal rate (rapid retries, abandon after wrong answer)
- Parent-reported confidence improvement (weekly check)

### Open questions for child playtest

- Which mini-game feels most fun vs most confusing?
- Does reward choice feel exciting or overwhelming?
- Is feedback wording clear after a mistake?
- Does the pet evolution pacing feel too slow or too fast?
- Which topic feels hardest and why?
- Are sessions truly short enough to feel “game-like”?
- Do visual cues make next steps obvious without adult help?
- What makes the child want to come back tomorrow?
